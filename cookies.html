Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website or use our mobile applications. They are widely used to make websites and apps work more efficiently and provide a better user experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

Smart Luck Games uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings;
  • Understand how you use our games and website;
  • Improve our games and services;
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements;
  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns;
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our games and website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, access to secure areas, and game functionality. Without these cookies, our services cannot work properly.

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how players interact with our games by collecting and reporting information anonymously. They help us improve our games' performance and user experience.

Functional Cookies

These cookies allow our games to remember choices you make (such as your language preference or game settings) and provide enhanced, more personal features.

Targeting/Advertising Cookies

These cookies may be set through our games by our advertising partners. They may be used to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party companies to place cookies on your device to:

  • Provide analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics);
  • Deliver targeted advertising;
  • Provide social media features;
  • Support customer service tools.

5. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers and mobile devices allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device;
  • Delete all cookies or specific cookies;
  • Block third-party cookies;
  • Block cookies from specific sites;
  • Block all cookies;
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser.

Please note that if you disable or delete cookies, some features of our games may not function properly.

6. Mobile Applications

Our mobile games may use technologies similar to cookies, including:

  • Local storage;
  • Device identifiers;
  • Software development kits (SDKs);
  • Application programming interfaces (APIs).

You can manage these through your device settings or by uninstalling our games.

7. Data Retention

The length of time cookies remain on your device depends on whether they are "persistent" or "session" cookies:

  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser or app;
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device until they expire or you delete them.

8. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our website.

9. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights regarding cookies and similar technologies, including:

  • The right to know what cookies are being used;
  • The right to opt-out of certain types of cookies;
  • The right to withdraw consent for cookie use.
