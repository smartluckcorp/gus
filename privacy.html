Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 14, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Smart Luck Corporation Limited ("we," "our," or "us") handles your data when you use our mobile application ("App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy outlines our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information from users of the App, nor do we utilize any analytics tools within the App. No user data, personal or non-personal, is collected, stored, or shared through this application.

  • No Personal Information: We do not request or store personal information such as your name, email address, payment details, or any other identifying information.
  • No Analytics Data Collection: Our App does not employ any analytics tools or services. No data is collected regarding app usage patterns, feature interactions, or performance metrics.
  • No Device Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable device information such as IP address, operating system, or device model.

Data Sharing

Since no data is collected from users, no information is shared with third parties. Our App is designed with privacy in mind, ensuring that all functionalities are available without any data tracking or sharing.

Data Retention

As no personal or analytics data is collected, no data is retained or stored from your interactions with the App.

Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the integrity of the App and ensure a safe user experience. These measures include regular security updates and adherence to standard security practices to safeguard against potential threats.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 18. If you believe your child has used the App and provided any personal information through other means, please contact us at chennhaiboo@gmail.com, and we will take steps to ensure no data is retained.

Your Rights and Choices

We respect your rights under applicable privacy laws. Although we do not collect any personal data, if you have any questions or concerns about your privacy while using the App, feel free to contact us at chennhaiboo@gmail.com.

International Data Transfers

As we do not collect, process, or store user data, no information is transferred internationally.

Legal Compliance

We are committed to complying with all applicable privacy laws and regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Although we do not collect personal data, we strive to ensure that our practices respect user privacy and legal obligations.
